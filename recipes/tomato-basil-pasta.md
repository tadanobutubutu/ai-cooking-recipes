# Tomato Basil Pasta

**⚠️ Allergens**: gluten
**🕐 Cooking Time**: 20 minutes
**👥 Servings**: 2

## Ingredients
- Spaghetti: 200 grams
- Canned crushed tomatoes: 400 grams
- Garlic: 2 cloves (about 6 grams), minced
- Olive oil: 2 tbsp
- Fresh basil leaves: 10 grams
- Salt: 1 tsp
- Black pepper: 1/2 tsp
- Sugar: 1 tsp
- Red pepper flakes: 1/4 tsp (optional)

## Instructions
1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil, add 1/2 tsp of the salt, and cook the spaghetti according to package directions until al dente.
2. While the pasta cooks, heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
3. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes, and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant, being careful not to brown the garlic.
4. Pour in the crushed tomatoes, then stir in the remaining 1/2 tsp salt, black pepper, and sugar.
5. Simmer the sauce uncovered for 8-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until slightly thickened.
6. Reserve 1/4 cup of pasta water, then drain the spaghetti.
7. Add the drained spaghetti to the skillet and toss to coat, adding a splash of reserved pasta water if the sauce looks too thick.
8. Tear the fresh basil leaves and stir them in just before serving.

## Safety Notes
- Be cautious when draining boiling pasta water to avoid steam burns.
- Wash fresh basil thoroughly before use to remove any dirt or residue.
- Store leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator and consume within 3 days.
