# Garlic Butter Chicken

**⚠️ Allergens**: dairy
**🕐 Cooking Time**: 20 minutes
**👥 Servings**: 2

## Ingredients
- Boneless, skinless chicken breast: 300 g
- Unsalted butter: 30 g
- Olive oil: 1 tbsp
- Garlic cloves, minced: 4 (about 12 g)
- Salt: 1 tsp
- Black pepper: 0.5 tsp
- Paprika: 0.5 tsp
- Lemon juice: 1 tbsp
- Fresh parsley, chopped: 1 tbsp

## Instructions
1. Pat the chicken breasts dry and slice them horizontally into 2 thinner cutlets each so they cook evenly.
2. Season both sides of the chicken with the salt, black pepper, and paprika.
3. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat until shimmering.
4. Add the chicken and sear for about 4 minutes per side, until golden brown and cooked through.
5. Reduce the heat to medium-low and add the butter and minced garlic to the skillet.
6. Spoon the melted garlic butter over the chicken for about 1 minute, being careful not to burn the garlic.
7. Turn off the heat, squeeze the lemon juice over the chicken, and sprinkle with chopped parsley.
8. Rest the chicken for 2 minutes before serving with the pan sauce spooned over the top.

## Safety Notes
- Cook chicken to an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C), verified with a meat thermometer at the thickest part.
- Wash hands, cutting boards, and utensils with hot soapy water immediately after contact with raw chicken to avoid cross-contamination.
- Refrigerate any leftovers within 2 hours and consume within 3 days.
