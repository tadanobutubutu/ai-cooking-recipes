# Spaghetti Carbonara

**⚠️ Allergens**: eggs / dairy / gluten
**🕐 Cooking Time**: 20 minutes
**👥 Servings**: 2

## Ingredients
- Spaghetti: 200g
- Guanciale or Pancetta: 100g
- Pecorino Romano cheese (grated): 50g
- Large eggs: 2
- Black pepper: 1 tsp
- Salt: 1 tsp

## Instructions
1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the spaghetti until al dente.
2. In a skillet, cook the diced guanciale over medium heat until crispy. Remove from heat.
3. In a bowl, whisk the eggs, grated Pecorino Romano, and black pepper together.
4. Drain the pasta, reserving 1/4 cup of pasta water.
5. Quickly toss the hot pasta in the skillet with the guanciale (off the heat).
6. Pour the egg and cheese mixture over the pasta, tossing rapidly to create a creamy sauce. Add pasta water if needed.
7. Serve immediately with extra cheese and black pepper.

## Safety Notes
- Consume immediately as the dish contains lightly cooked eggs. Use pasteurized eggs if concerned.
