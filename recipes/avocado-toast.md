# Avocado Toast

**⚠️ Allergens**: gluten
**🕐 Cooking Time**: 10 minutes
**👥 Servings**: 2

## Ingredients
- Bread (sourdough or whole wheat): 2 slices
- Ripe avocado: 1 medium
- Lemon juice: 1 tsp
- Salt: 1/4 tsp
- Black pepper: 1/4 tsp
- Red pepper flakes: 1/4 tsp
- Olive oil: 1 tsp

## Instructions
1. Toast the bread slices until golden brown and crispy.
2. Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
3. Add the lemon juice, salt, and black pepper to the avocado.
4. Mash the avocado mixture with a fork until it reaches your desired consistency.
5. Spread the mashed avocado evenly over the toasted bread slices.
6. Top with red pepper flakes and a drizzle of olive oil.

## Safety Notes
- Be careful when cutting the avocado and removing the pit to avoid hand injuries.
- Toast bread carefully to avoid burning or fire hazards.
