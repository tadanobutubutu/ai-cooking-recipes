# Beef Tacos

**⚠️ Allergens**: dairy
**🕐 Cooking Time**: 20 minutes
**👥 Servings**: 2

## Ingredients
- Ground beef: 400 grams
- Taco seasoning: 2 tbsp
- Olive oil: 1 tbsp
- Hard taco shells: 6 shells
- Shredded lettuce: 100 grams
- Diced tomatoes: 100 grams
- Shredded cheese: 100 grams
- Sour cream: 2 tbsp

## Instructions
1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
2. Add the ground beef and cook until browned, about 5-7 minutes.
3. Drain excess fat from the skillet.
4. Stir in the taco seasoning and 1/4 cup of water, simmering for 3 minutes.
5. Warm the taco shells in an oven at 350°F (175°C) for 3-5 minutes.
6. Assemble the tacos by filling shells with the beef mixture.
7. Top with lettuce, tomatoes, shredded cheese, and a dollop of sour cream.

## Safety Notes
- Cook ground beef to an internal temperature of 160°F (71°C).
