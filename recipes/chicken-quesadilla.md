# Chicken Quesadilla

**⚠️ Allergens**: dairy / gluten
**🕐 Cooking Time**: 15 minutes
**👥 Servings**: 2

## Ingredients
- Flour tortillas: 2 large
- Cooked chicken breast, shredded: 150 grams
- Cheddar cheese, shredded: 100 grams
- Monterey Jack cheese, shredded: 100 grams
- Butter: 1 tbsp

## Instructions
1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and melt half of the butter.
2. Place one tortilla in the skillet.
3. Sprinkle half of the mixed cheeses evenly over the tortilla.
4. Distribute the shredded chicken evenly over the cheese.
5. Sprinkle the remaining cheese over the chicken.
6. Top with the second tortilla.
7. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the bottom tortilla is golden brown and crispy.
8. Carefully flip the quesadilla using a large spatula, adding the remaining butter to the skillet if needed.
9. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until the second side is golden brown and the cheese is fully melted.
10. Remove from the skillet, let it rest for a minute, and slice into wedges.

## Safety Notes
- Be careful when flipping the quesadilla as the hot filling might spill.
