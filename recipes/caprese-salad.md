# Caprese Salad

**⚠️ Allergens**: dairy
**🕐 Cooking Time**: 10 minutes
**👥 Servings**: 2

## Ingredients
- Fresh mozzarella cheese: 250 g
- Ripe tomatoes: 350 g
- Fresh basil leaves: 15 g
- Extra virgin olive oil: 2 tbsp
- Balsamic glaze: 1 tbsp
- Salt: 0.5 tsp
- Black pepper: 0.25 tsp

## Instructions
1. Slice the tomatoes and fresh mozzarella into 1/4-inch thick slices.
2. Arrange the tomato and mozzarella slices on a serving platter, alternating them and overlapping slightly.
3. Tuck the fresh basil leaves between the slices of tomato and mozzarella.
4. Drizzle the extra virgin olive oil and balsamic glaze evenly over the salad.
5. Season with salt and black pepper just before serving.

## Safety Notes
- Wash the tomatoes and basil leaves thoroughly under running water before use.
