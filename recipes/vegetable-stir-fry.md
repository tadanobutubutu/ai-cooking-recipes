# Vegetable Stir Fry

**⚠️ Allergens**: soy
**🕐 Cooking Time**: 15 minutes
**👥 Servings**: 2

## Ingredients
- Broccoli florets: 150 grams
- Red bell pepper: 100 grams
- Carrots: 100 grams
- Snow peas: 50 grams
- Soy sauce: 2 tbsp
- Sesame oil: 1 tbsp
- Minced garlic: 1 tsp
- Minced ginger: 1 tsp

## Instructions
1. Heat sesame oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Add minced garlic and ginger, cooking until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
3. Add broccoli and carrots, stir-frying for 4 to 5 minutes.
4. Add red bell pepper and snow peas, cooking for another 3 minutes.
5. Pour in the soy sauce and toss the vegetables until well-coated and tender-crisp.
6. Remove from heat and serve hot immediately.

## Safety Notes
- Wash all fresh produce thoroughly under running water before preparing.
