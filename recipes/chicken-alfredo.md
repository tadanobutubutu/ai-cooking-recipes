# Chicken Alfredo

**⚠️ Allergens**: dairy / gluten
**🕐 Cooking Time**: 25 minutes
**👥 Servings**: 2

## Ingredients
- Fettuccine pasta: 200g
- Chicken breast: 250g
- Unsalted butter: 50g
- Heavy cream: 200ml
- Parmesan cheese, grated: 50g
- Garlic, minced: 1 tbsp
- Olive oil: 1 tbsp
- Salt: 1/2 tsp
- Black pepper: 1/4 tsp

## Instructions
1. Boil a large pot of salted water. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
2. Cut the chicken breast into bite-sized pieces and season with a pinch of salt and pepper.
3. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook until golden brown and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
4. In the same skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 1 minute until fragrant.
5. Reduce the heat to low and pour in the heavy cream. Simmer gently for 2-3 minutes until slightly thickened.
6. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth. Season the sauce with salt and black pepper to taste.
7. Add the cooked pasta and chicken back into the skillet. Toss everything together until the pasta is evenly coated in the creamy sauce.
8. Serve hot, garnished with extra Parmesan cheese or fresh parsley if desired.

## Safety Notes
- Cook chicken to an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C).
