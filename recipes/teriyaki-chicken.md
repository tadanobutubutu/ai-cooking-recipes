# Teriyaki Chicken

**⚠️ Allergens**: soy / gluten
**🕐 Cooking Time**: 20 minutes
**👥 Servings**: 2

## Ingredients
- Chicken thighs, boneless and skinless: 400g
- Soy sauce: 3 tbsp
- Mirin: 2 tbsp
- Sugar: 1 tbsp
- Sake (or water): 2 tbsp
- Vegetable oil: 1 tbsp
- Sesame seeds (optional): 1 tsp

## Instructions
1. In a small bowl, mix the soy sauce, mirin, sugar, and sake until the sugar is mostly dissolved. This is your teriyaki sauce.
2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
3. Add the chicken thighs to the skillet and cook for about 5-7 minutes on each side, or until browned and cooked through.
4. Pour the teriyaki sauce over the chicken in the skillet.
5. Reduce the heat to medium-low and simmer for about 3-5 minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken and glaze the chicken. Turn the chicken occasionally to coat it evenly.
6. Remove from heat. Slice the chicken and serve warm, garnished with sesame seeds if desired.

## Safety Notes
- Cook chicken to an internal temperature of 165°F (75°C).
